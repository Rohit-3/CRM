# Enterprise CRM Platform - User Guide

## Overview

Welcome to the Enterprise CRM Platform - a comprehensive customer relationship management system designed to help businesses manage their sales pipeline, track customer interactions, and drive revenue growth.

## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Create Your Account**
   - Visit the login page
   - Click on the "Sign Up" tab
   - Enter your full name, choose a username, and create a password
   - **Important**: The first user to register automatically becomes the system administrator

2. **Login**
   - Use your username (without @miaoda.com) and password
   - The system will remember your session

## Core Features

### Dashboard

Your central hub for CRM insights:
- **Key Metrics**: View total leads, contacts, opportunities, and revenue at a glance
- **Performance Indicators**: Track won/lost deals, conversion rates, and average deal size
- **Pipeline Overview**: See opportunities distributed across different stages
- **Task Management**: Monitor overdue and upcoming tasks
- **Quick Actions**: Rapidly create new leads, contacts, opportunities, or tasks

### Leads Management

Capture and nurture potential customers:
- **Create Leads**: Add new prospects with contact information, company details, and source tracking
- **Lead Scoring**: Assign scores (0-100) to prioritize high-value prospects
- **Status Tracking**: Monitor leads through stages: New → Contacted → Qualified/Unqualified
- **Source Attribution**: Track where leads come from (website, referral, social media, etc.)
- **Search & Filter**: Quickly find leads by name, email, or company

### Contacts Management

Maintain your customer relationships:
- **Contact Profiles**: Store comprehensive information including name, title, department, and multiple contact methods
- **Organization**: Link contacts to their companies for better context
- **Communication History**: Track all interactions with each contact
- **Quick Actions**: Email or call directly from the contact record

### Accounts Management

Manage company relationships:
- **Company Profiles**: Store business information including industry, location, and website
- **Relationship Tracking**: See all contacts and opportunities associated with each account
- **Search Capabilities**: Find companies by name, industry, or location

### Opportunities (Pipeline)

Track deals from prospect to close:
- **Deal Stages**: Move opportunities through your sales process
  - Prospecting
  - Qualification
  - Proposal
  - Negotiation
  - Closed Won/Lost
- **Financial Tracking**: Record deal values and expected close dates
- **Probability Assessment**: Assign win probability percentages
- **Status Management**: Mark deals as open, won, or lost

### Tasks

Stay organized and never miss a follow-up:
- **Task Creation**: Set up to-dos with titles, descriptions, and due dates
- **Priority Levels**: Mark tasks as high, medium, or low priority
- **Status Tracking**: Monitor pending and completed tasks
- **Overdue Alerts**: Visual indicators for tasks that need immediate attention
- **Quick Completion**: Mark tasks as done with one click

### Reports & Analytics

Make data-driven decisions:
- **Sales Performance**: View won/lost deals, conversion rates, and average deal sizes
- **Pipeline Analysis**: See deal distribution across stages with total values
- **Lead Source Analysis**: Understand which channels generate the most leads
- **Key Metrics**: Track overall CRM health with comprehensive statistics

### Admin Panel (Administrators Only)

Manage your team and system:
- **User Management**: View all registered users
- **Role Assignment**: Assign roles to team members:
  - **Admin**: Full system access and user management
  - **Sales Manager**: Team oversight and reporting
  - **Sales Rep**: Lead and opportunity management
  - **Marketing**: Campaign and lead generation focus
  - **Support**: Customer service and ticket management
  - **Executive**: Read-only access to reports and analytics
- **System Statistics**: Monitor user counts and team composition

## User Roles & Permissions

### Admin
- Full access to all features
- Can manage user roles
- Access to admin panel
- Can view and edit all data

### Sales Manager
- View team performance
- Access all sales data
- Create and manage opportunities
- Generate reports

### Sales Rep
- Manage own leads and opportunities
- Create and update contacts
- Track tasks and activities
- View own performance metrics

### Marketing
- Create and manage leads
- View campaign performance
- Access to lead source analytics

### Support
- Access customer contact information
- Create and manage tasks
- Log customer interactions

### Executive
- Read-only access to dashboards
- View all reports and analytics
- Monitor overall CRM performance

## Best Practices

### Lead Management
1. **Capture Leads Promptly**: Add new prospects as soon as they show interest
2. **Score Consistently**: Use a standard scoring system across your team
3. **Update Status Regularly**: Keep lead status current to reflect actual progress
4. **Track Sources**: Always record where leads come from for ROI analysis

### Opportunity Management
1. **Keep Deals Updated**: Move opportunities through stages as they progress
2. **Set Realistic Close Dates**: Update expected close dates as situations change
3. **Record Deal Values**: Accurate amounts help with forecasting
4. **Document Why Deals Are Lost**: Learn from losses to improve win rates

### Task Management
1. **Set Due Dates**: Every task should have a deadline
2. **Prioritize Wisely**: Use priority levels to focus on what matters most
3. **Complete Promptly**: Mark tasks done as soon as they're finished
4. **Review Daily**: Check your task list each morning

### Data Quality
1. **Complete Profiles**: Fill in as much information as possible
2. **Avoid Duplicates**: Search before creating new records
3. **Update Regularly**: Keep contact information current
4. **Use Consistent Formatting**: Standardize how you enter data

## Tips for Success

1. **Start Your Day with the Dashboard**: Review key metrics and tasks each morning
2. **Use Search Effectively**: Save time by using the search function instead of scrolling
3. **Leverage Quick Actions**: Use dashboard quick actions for faster data entry
4. **Review Reports Weekly**: Check analytics to identify trends and opportunities
5. **Keep Tasks Current**: Update task status throughout the day
6. **Document Interactions**: Log all customer communications for team visibility

## Support & Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't Login**
- Verify your username is correct (no @miaoda.com needed)
- Check that Caps Lock is off
- Ensure password is at least 6 characters

**Don't See Expected Data**
- Check your user role permissions
- Verify you're looking at the correct filters
- Refresh the page to load latest data

**Need to Change User Role**
- Only administrators can change roles
- Contact your system admin for role changes
- You cannot change your own role

### Getting Help

- Contact your system administrator for access issues
- Check the dashboard for system status
- Review this guide for feature explanations

## Security Notes

- Never share your login credentials
- Log out when using shared computers
- Administrators should regularly review user access
- First registered user becomes admin - secure this account carefully
